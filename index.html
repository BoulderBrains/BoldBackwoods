About the artist

Nate Colton is an artist based out of Providence, Rhode Island. Growing up in New England in the 80's and 90's, he was inspired by nature and hip hop at a young age.

Trained in drawing and painting, Nate later discovered pyrography. He now experiments with different materials and techniques, combining drawing and painting with fine art pyrography. His two favorite things, wildlife and hip hop, are consistent themes throughout his art.

This is Bold Backwoods. A little bit woodsy, a little bit street.
